About Us

OpenTech is an event dedicated to showcasing and explaining open-source tools, where participants have the opportunity to exhibit their own open-source creations, provide live demonstrations of their tools, and present projects that exemplify the utility and versatility of these tools. It serves as a vibrant hub for knowledge sharing, and the promotion of open-source software principles, bringing together developers, enthusiasts, and users to celebrate the open-source ecosystem's innovation and community-driven spirit.
